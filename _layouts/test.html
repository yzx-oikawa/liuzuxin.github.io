I am a first-year Ph.D. student advised by Prof. Ding Zhao in Safe AI Lab. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University in Jul, 2018. Before coming to CMU, I also spent one year in Tsinghua University researching on Acoustic Event Detection with Prof. Liang He.

I am interested in deep generative models and rare event simulation, especially to generate safety-critical scenarios for autonomous driving. I also follow multiple tracks of robotics, such as robot perception, motion planning and navigation.
